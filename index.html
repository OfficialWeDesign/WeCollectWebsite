WeCollect

Gives life better price.

WeCollect is an application created by the WeDesign company that solves the problem of inflation by showing the prices of the same items in different stores.

Users themselves help each other because they have access to a barcode scanner with which they enter a specific item into the application along with its actual price in the store.
